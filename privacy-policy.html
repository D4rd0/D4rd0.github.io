Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 2026

This Privacy Policy explains what personal data Dressy ("the App") collects, why, and what rights you have. We process personal data in accordance with the EU General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR), as the developer is based in Spain.

1. Data we collect

  • Account data: a unique user ID; if you sign in with Google, your email address.
  • Photos & generated images: the reference photo you upload, your generated digital twin, and try-on results.
  • App data: your saved wardrobe items, try-on history, country and language preferences, dark mode setting, and remaining Credits.
  • Technical data: basic device/app logs needed to operate and secure the service.

2. Why we process it (purpose & legal basis)

  • To create your account and provide the core features (Art. 6(1)(b) GDPR - performance of a contract with you).
  • To generate your avatar and try-on images via our AI provider (contract performance / your explicit action each time).
  • To remember your preferences and Credits balance (contract performance).
  • To keep the service secure and prevent abuse (Art. 6(1)(f) - legitimate interest).

3. Who we share data with

  • Google (Gemini AI) - processes your uploaded photo and generated images to produce results; also provides Google Sign-In if you choose that login method.
  • Supabase - our backend provider (database, file storage, authentication); hosts all account, wardrobe, history and image data.
  • AliExpress - if you tap a product to view or buy it, you are taken to AliExpress; we do not share your photos or account data with AliExpress, only standard affiliate tracking parameters.

We do not sell your personal data.

4. International transfers

Some of the providers above (Google, Supabase) may process data outside the European Economic Area. Where this happens, they rely on appropriate safeguards such as the EU Standard Contractual Clauses.

5. Data retention

We keep your data for as long as your account exists. If you delete your account, your photos, generated images, wardrobe, and history are deleted; some minimal records may be kept where required by law.

6. Your rights (GDPR)

You have the right to access, rectify, delete ("right to be forgotten"), restrict, or port your personal data, and to object to certain processing. To exercise any of these rights, contact us at projecttitanlabs@protonmail.com. You also have the right to lodge a complaint with your local data protection authority (in Spain, the AEPD).

7. Account & data deletion

You can delete your account and all associated data at any time directly in the App (Profile -> Advanced Options -> Delete Account), or by contacting projecttitanlabs@protonmail.com. We aim to process deletion requests promptly.

8. Children's privacy

The App is not directed at children under 16. We do not knowingly collect personal data from children under that age.

9. Security

We use industry-standard measures (encrypted storage, access-controlled databases via Row Level Security) to protect your data, but no method of transmission or storage is 100% secure.

10. Changes to this policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time; material changes will be reflected by updating the "Last updated" date above.

11. Contact

Questions or requests about your data: projecttitanlabs@protonmail.com
