Dressy - Account Deletion

Last updated: July 2026

How to request deletion of your Dressy account

You can delete your Dressy account and all associated data at any time, directly within the app:

  1. Open the Dressy app and sign in.
  2. Go to the Profile tab.
  3. Tap Advanced Options.
  4. Tap Delete Account, confirm the warning, and type DRESSY to confirm.

Your account and all associated data are deleted immediately - there is no waiting period for in-app deletion.

Can't access the app?

If you no longer have access to the app or your device, you can request deletion by emailing projecttitanlabs@protonmail.com from the email address associated with your Dressy account (the Google account you signed in with). Include your Google account email in the message. We process these requests within 30 days.

What gets deleted

  • Your account and profile (unique user ID, email, country and language preferences, dark mode setting)
  • Your uploaded reference photo
  • Your generated digital twin (virtual avatar) image
  • All virtual try-on result images
  • Your saved wardrobe items
  • Your try-on history
  • Your Credits balance and purchase records stored in our database

This is a permanent, immediate deletion - we do not retain a backup or soft-deleted copy of any of the above after your account is deleted.

What may be retained, and for how long

We do not retain any of your personal data in our own systems after deletion. However, some data may still exist outside our systems, governed by the retention policies of the respective third party:

  • Google may retain its own sign-in/account records according to Google's own retention policy, independent of Dressy.
  • RevenueCat (our in-app purchase processor) and the Google Play Store may retain transaction records for a period required for financial, tax, or fraud-prevention compliance, independent of Dressy.
  • AliExpress retains its own order records for any purchases you completed directly on AliExpress; Dressy never had access to those records in the first place.

None of the above third-party records are accessible to us and are not linked back to your deleted Dressy account.
